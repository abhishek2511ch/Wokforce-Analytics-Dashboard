# Wokforce-Analytics-Dashboard

Developed an interactive HR dashboard in Power BI to analyze workforce distribution and key
HR metrics.  
• Visualized employee demographics including gender ratio (60% male, 40% female) and total
  headcount (1470).  
• Analyzed promotion eligibility, identifying 5% employees due for promotion and 95% not due.  
• Created insights on employee status (92% active, 8% retrenched) to track workforce stability.   
• Built visualizations for service years and job levels to understand experience distribution and   
  hierarchy.   
• Designed a user-friendly and visually structured dashboard for quick decisionmaking by HR  
  teams.
